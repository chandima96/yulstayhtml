FAMILY

Trust over 5 decades of hard work, integrity, and vision passed down from Founders Hermann and Anneliese Rumpf, parents to Karsten Rumpf and his wife Bettina Rumpf, to their sons Richard and Daniel Rumpf. Prime Properties has been innovating for 3 generations, by surpassing themselves when meticulously transforming and creating beautiful highly sought after spaces.

The Rumpf family brings life to places through refurbishment and managerial strategies. Our long-term involvement increases value across the board, making our socially-responsible outlook and practices profitable for all. Our approach is simple: fashion quality design-driven spaces to suit contemporary needs.
